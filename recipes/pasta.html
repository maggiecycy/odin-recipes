Description

his pasta dish is a classic Italian comfort food, made with a rich tomato sauce and perfectly cooked noodles

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the spaghetti until al dente and drain.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and saut√© garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add tomato sauce, salt, and pepper; simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Mix in the pasta and toss well.
  5. Serve hot with Parmesan cheese on top.
