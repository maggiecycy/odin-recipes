Description

This traditional Bolognese sauce, or “ragù alla bolognese,” is a slow-cooked Italian meat sauce made with ground beef, vegetables, and tomatoes.

Its perfect for serving over tagliatelle or spaghetti, giving your meal a rich and savory flavor that’s authentically Italian.

Ingredients

  • 400g ground beef
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 chopped carrots
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • 2 cups tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • Olive oil, salt, and pepper
  • 1 cup milk

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan and sauté onion, carrot, and celery until soft.
  2. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  3. Pour in red wine and let it reduce slightly.
  4. Add tomato puree, salt, and pepper. Simmer on low heat for at least 1 hour.
  5. Stir in milk at the end for a creamy finish.
  6. Serve over pasta with grated Parmesan cheese.
