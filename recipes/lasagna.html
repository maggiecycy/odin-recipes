Description

This lasagna is rich, creamy, and layered with cheese, tomato sauce, and tender pasta sheets. It’s the perfect comfort meal for weekends.

Its a dish that combines the best of Italian cooking: hearty, cheesy, and deeply satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Olive oil, salt, and pepper

Steps

  1. Boil lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, cook ground beef with onion and garlic until browned.
  3. Add tomato sauce, salt, and pepper; simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. In a baking dish, layer noodles, sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella. Repeat layers.
  5. Top with Parmesan and bake at 180°C for 30 minutes.
  6. Let cool slightly before serving.
